# Privacy Policy — SondeR cat

_Last updated: 2026_

SondeR cat is a free, open-source desktop pet application. This policy
explains, in plain language, what data the app touches and where it goes.
The short version: **SondeR cat does not collect, transmit, or sell your
personal data, and it has no analytics or telemetry of any kind.**

## What SondeR cat stores on your computer

The app saves your settings locally on your own PC (in your user profile,
e.g. `%APPDATA%`), including your chosen cat name, appearance, and — if you
use the assistant — your AI API key. This information stays on your machine.
It is never uploaded to the project's authors or to any server operated by
the project. You can delete it at any time by removing the app's settings
file.

## The optional AI assistant

SondeR cat includes an **optional** assistant feature that is **off until you
choose to set it up** by entering your own third-party AI API key (for
example, a Google Gemini key that you obtain yourself).

When — and only when — you actively use this feature:

- The text of the question you ask is sent directly from your computer to the
  AI provider (e.g. Google) using **your own API key**, in order to generate
  an answer.
- If you explicitly enable the optional "screen check" feature and then ask a
  question about your screen, a single screenshot is captured and sent to the
  AI provider for that one answer. Screenshots are **not stored** and are
  **not retained** in the app after the answer is returned.
- These requests go **directly to the AI provider you chose**. The project's
  authors do not operate any intermediary server, do not receive your
  questions, screenshots, or key, and have no access to this data.

Your use of a third-party AI provider is governed by **that provider's own
privacy policy and terms** (for example, Google's). Please review the policy
of whichever provider's API key you supply.

## What SondeR cat does NOT do

- ❌ No analytics, tracking, or telemetry
- ❌ No user accounts, no sign-in
- ❌ No collection of personal data by the project or its authors
- ❌ No advertising
- ❌ No selling or sharing of any data

## Updates

The app may check the project's public repository for new versions in order
to update itself. This is a normal request to a public download location and
does not send any personal information about you.
